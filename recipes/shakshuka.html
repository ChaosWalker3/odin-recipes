Shakshuka

Image from Ben on Allrecipes

Shakshuka is a traditional staple from Tunisia.

It consists of poached eggs in a spicy tomato sauce with onions, bell pepper, and garlic.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons Olive oil
  • 1 & 1/3 cups Chopped onion
  • 1 cup Thinly sliced bell peppers, any color
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced or to taste
  • 2 & 1/2 cups Chopped tomatoes
  • 1 Hot chili pepper, seeded and finely chopped, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon Paprika
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 4 Large eggs

Steps

  1. Mix the tomatoes, chili pepper, cumin paprika, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Heat up a skillet in medium heat. Put enough oil to somewhat cover the bottom.
  3. Put the onions, bell peppers, and garlic. Cook and stir until they have softened with the onions having turned translucent.
  4. Put the tomato mixture into the skillet and let simmer uncovered for around 10 minutes.
  5. Make 4 indentations in the mixture and crack the eggs into each one. Cover afterwards and cook for around 5 minutes or when the eggs are firm enough however not too dry.
  6. Serve.
