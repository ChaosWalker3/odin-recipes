Hash Browns

Image from Smittenkitchen

Hash Browns are a popular breakfast dish in America.

It is made of finely-julienned or shredded potatoes that are fried until golden brown.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs Russett potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon Potato starch or Cornstarch
  • Olive oil or any high-heat oil to fry
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into chunks.
  2. Put the potato chunks into a boiling pot of water. Wait for 15 minutes or so.
  3. Check if soft enough to easily cut through but not so much that it is mushy.
  4. When done, drain the water and move the potatoes on to a cutting board. Let it cool slightly for around 5 minutes.
  5. Cut them or julienne into little bits.
  6. Put them into a bowl and put in the starch and then season to taste with salt and pepper, but only halfway. Shape them into patty-like structures.
  7. Heat up a large frying pan and put enough oil to cover the patties. When oil is hot, add the potato patties, enough at a time.
  8. Cook for 1-2 minutes. If the patties brown quickly, reduce heat.
  9. When patties are golden brown, move to a plate lined with paper towels to absorb the excess oil. Sprinkle the other half of seasonings immediately.
  10. Serve.
